Description:

Overhaul is a 2d platformer a few peers and I made for the 2020 global game jam. The game was made in 48 hours and was a great exercise in teamwork and time crunch. Overhaul is a game about a character who has lost and must retrieve some of his mechanical augmentations. The theme of the game jam was repair
