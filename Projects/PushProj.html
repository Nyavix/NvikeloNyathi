Push

Status: Active

Start Date: May 2019

End Date: To Be Determined

Currently no playable demo

Description:

Push is a 2.5d run and gun platformer that focuses on momentum. Through parkour and the propulsion that the weapons give, the main character, Hannibal makes his way through a dystopian city to reach his girlfriend on the other side. Biotech infused AI has decided humankind is detrimental to the survival of the earth and has taken it upon himself to eradicate all human beings.
